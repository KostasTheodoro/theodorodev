<SectionHeading icon="problem">The problem</SectionHeading>
Artemis wanted to launch a pre-order e-shop for her business importing handmade
furniture from Bali. She needed a professional, modern site that could showcase
her full range of pre-order products, along with live chat for questions about
how pre-ordering works.
<SectionHeading icon="built">What I built</SectionHeading>
In the early stages, this was a plain Next.js and React site with Tailwind CSS
and Headless UI, with no database due to the tight timeline we had to get it
live. The project is still evolving in 2026: we've since integrated Supabase and
Sanity CMS to fully automate how new products are added and stored. It started
as a collaboration with another developer in the early stages, and has been a
solo project ever since, one I'm still actively improving.
