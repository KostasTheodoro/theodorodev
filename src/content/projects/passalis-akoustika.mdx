## The problem

Passalis Akoustika, an audiology practice in Marousi, needed a site that clearly explains their services — free hearing checks, custom-fitted hearing aids, EOPYY reimbursement — and makes it easy for patients to get in touch and book a visit.

## What I built

A clean, trustworthy site laying out their services and product range, with clear calls to action to book a free hearing check.
