<SectionHeading icon="problem">The problem</SectionHeading>

Ilias wanted to bring his family business into the digital age. Passalis Akoustika is a hearing aid clinic in Marousi, partnered with some of the largest hearing aid providers in Greece. I was brought on to build a simple informational site that both younger users and older patients could navigate with ease. SEO was a priority from the start, so the business could finally have the digital presence it deserved.

<SectionHeading icon="built">What I built</SectionHeading>

A clean, modern site, originally built with Next.js and migrating to Astro in 2026 for even faster performance. It covers everything Ilias needed, page by page, but the real highlight is the SEO work: within a short time, the site was loading noticeably faster than competitors and started genuinely competing with them in search results. The site stays intentionally simple and informational, with just a contact form and phone number for booking, exactly as Ilias wanted.
