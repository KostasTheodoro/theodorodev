## The problem

GArts teaches digital creative skills — 3D modeling, digital sculpting, animation, rendering — to visual creators, storytellers, and digital communicators. The site needed to feel as creative and fast as the courses themselves, with clear paths for different types of learners instead of a generic, cluttered course catalog.

## What I built

A fast, modern site built around GArts' "edutainment" positioning: clear course categories, quick load times, and a design that stays out of the way of the content.
