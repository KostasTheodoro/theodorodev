## The problem

Giorgos teaches digital creative skills like 3D modeling, animation, and rendering. He needed a website that felt as creative and visually striking as his own work, one that made it clear who he is and what he offers. He also wanted a custom booking flow so clients could easily schedule in-person or remote lessons, and since his audience includes both Greek and international clients, the site needed to work in both languages.

## What I built

A fast, modern site built around Giorgos' own visuals and animations, with clear navigation that gives every section a purpose. The site is fully responsive, fully bilingual (Greek/English), and includes a custom booking modal with a free Cal.com integration, tailored to fit his session types perfectly.
