## The problem

Blue Wind Apartments needed a site that sells the experience of staying in western Crete — not just a list of amenities — while linking cleanly out to their Booking.com and Airbnb listings.

## What I built

A visual, fast-loading site built around the apartments' seaside setting, with clear links to book directly through Booking.com and Airbnb.
