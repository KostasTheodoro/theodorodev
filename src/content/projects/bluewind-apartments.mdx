<SectionHeading icon="problem">The problem</SectionHeading>

Haris needed a modern, informational site for his rental apartments in Kissamos, Crete, linking out to Booking.com and Airbnb for actual reservations. The site had existed on Wix for years, and I was brought in to modernize it and add new features while keeping everything that already worked.

<SectionHeading icon="built">What I built</SectionHeading>

A modern informational site that carries over everything from the old Wix version, with a much better showcase of the rooms themselves. It links cleanly out to Booking.com and Airbnb, and includes a contact page so guests can reach out directly with questions. There's a booking form planned for the future, along with a full SPA for booking management, eventually moving away from Booking.com and Airbnb entirely. Like the others, this one keeps evolving.
